Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere was warmer during the time period that this data was generated. This would indicate that it was closer to summer in the Southern Hemisphere and winter in the Northern.

This also shows that there no data points below -60 degrees while there are quite a few data points above 60 degrees.
